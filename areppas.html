The Problem

Areppas spent some considerable time building a name in the gastronomic scene of New York city to the point they wanted to expand their reach online. For instance, the fact of being online gave users the possibility to check on reviews to take a final decision whether to eat there. But not only that, Areppa was looking forward to putting together a delivery service which they wanted to incorporate to their website.

RThe Solution

In this occasion we achieved a website design which conjugated with shapes, forms and colors of the actual dishes. The website is thorough, it shows information about the brand, its origins, their contribution to society, reviews and even a mailing list. However, the most interesting feature of this web page is we gave customers the possibility to order whatever they want, from wherever they are and have the food to go or delivered.
